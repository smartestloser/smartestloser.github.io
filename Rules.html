THE SMARTEST LOSER RULES

Voluntarily complete the initial weigh-in and all weekly weigh-ins to be eligible for prizes

Keep track (weekly) of the total points accumulated for each category (EXERCISE, NUTRITION, WATER), and body weight percentage and body fat, in the Smartest Loser Booklet and also with the website tracking tool!

Each week, transfer your results to the Smartest Loser electronic form emailed to you from the Wellness Team.

How to earn points?

  • Exercise - We're tracking the minutes you exercise this year. Get at least 20 minutes of heart-rate elevated movement in a day (140 points max can be earned a week)
  • Nutrition - 2 cups of fruit, 3 cups of veggies a day (4 points per day)
  • Water - 64 oz a day (2 points per day)
  • Body Fat % % Lost - Difference in body fat percentages each week - calculated as a loss percentage *
  • Weight % Loss - Difference in % body weight from lowest recorded weight *
    • * calculated as a loss percentage (You will be scored on either body fat % OR weight loss, whichever is greater)
