Exercise

Smartest Loser Criteria

EXERCISE: Participants need to engage in physical activity every week. Earn a minimum of 20 points per day. 1 point per 1 minute of exercise (minimum 20 points per day to a maximum 60 points per day) - Weekly cap is 140 points.
